💡 Messages as a Warm Audience

⚠️ Cost per Message

2,365 EGP / 305 messages = ~7.8 EGP per message. If your sales team converts 5% of conversations, that's ~156 EGP CPP from messages. Compare to Sales Website CPP to decide which is better.

Section 8

⚠️ Identified Problems

🔴 Problem 1: Post Engagement burns 33% of budget

  • 33,524 EGP spent with 0 purchase conversions
  • No clear ROI link to sales funnel
  • Reallocating to Sales Website = ~184 extra purchases/month

🔴 Problem 2: Funnel drop-offs

  • VC → ATC: only 8.4% (should be 15–25%)
  • ATC → Checkout: only 19.4% (should be 60–75%)
  • Checkout → Purchase: only 46.1% (should be 65–80%)

🔴 Problem 3: High Frequency (1.53x)

  • Same audience seeing same ads too often
  • Risk of ad fatigue → rising CPM and falling CTR
  • Need fresh creatives and/or audience expansion

🔴 Problem 4: No retargeting stack

  • 5,410 cart abandoners with no follow-up
  • 305 messages leads not retargeted for purchase
  • PE video viewers not funneled into sales campaigns
Section 9

💡 Strategic Insights & Recommendations

🚀 Priority 1: Scale "Menna Omran Video"

Highest ROAS in the account. Increase budget +30–50% every 3 days while ROAS stays above 6x. Test in new audiences (LLK 1–3%).

🚀 Priority 2: Duplicate Top CBO Campaigns

"Prospecting_CBO" and "Prospecting_CBO – Copy" are both winners. Create a 3rd duplicate with a new creative set to extend performance.

⛔ Priority 3: Pause or Justify Post Engagement April

31,529 EGP with 0 purchases. If this is for social proof/follower growth — set a KPI and cap the budget. If for sales — pause immediately.

🔁 Priority 4: Build Retargeting Campaigns

ATC retargeting (7d) + Checkout retargeting (3d) + Messages audience retargeting. These are near-buyers — lowest CPP to convert.

📹 Priority 5: New Video Creatives

Frequency 1.53x signals saturation. Produce 3–5 new video ads monthly. Use: UGC, before/after, product demos, social proof.

📊 Priority 6: Track Message-to-Purchase Rate

Add purchase tracking to the Messages funnel. If your team closes >10% of conversations, Sales Messages may be undervalued. If <5%, reallocate to Sales Website.
